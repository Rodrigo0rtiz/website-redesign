Who We Are

Northwest's Premier FBO

Bellingham Aviation Services has been the full-service FBO at Bellingham International Airport since January 1, 2004. We deliver superior service with warm, personalized attention — the kind you simply won't find at larger metro airports.

Whether you're transiting to Alaska and the Pacific Rim, hopping across the border to Vancouver, or exploring the beauty of the San Juan Islands, KBLI is your ideal base of operations.
